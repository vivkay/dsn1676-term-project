An illustrated introduction to HTML & CSS for absolute beginners.

Learn
HTML!

HTML is like the skeleton of your website. It provides the structure, and starts with an overall <body> element that can be then divided into three basic bits: header, main and footer!

< Click >

Click one of the panels on the side to learn more.

< header >

The entire body of your HTML code starts with a < header > element. This is where you put the face of your website: the attractive billboard, descriptive statement and maybe a call-to-action button to prompt visitor interaction!

< main >

The < main > part of your code is where you put your core information. This is where you'll find text and links and images–and sometimes some fun animations and transitions! This is where you put the heart and soul of your bunny…I mean website.

< footer >

Your website will end with a < footer > element. This is where you put your copyright information, date and sometimes your contact information. You might also want to add social media links and, if your site is rather large, another navigational menu to help your visitors find the juiciest parts of your site!
